## 11. Conclusion

The Sharikni project has a diverse stakeholder ecosystem with varying levels of interest and influence. The primary focus must be on satisfying critical stakeholders (developer, supervisor, university) while keeping high-interest stakeholders (end users, test users) informed and engaged. Success depends on:

1. **Clear communication** with supervisor and university
2. **Disciplined self-management** as developer
3. **User-centered design** for end users
4. **Professional quality** for future employers
5. **Social impact** for broader community

By understanding and managing these relationships effectively, the project can achieve both academic success and real-world impact.
